About Us

Discover Dundonian is not merely a list of the most popular tourist destinations in Dundee. We aim to connect people to the unique culture of Dundee, just as the people who live here (Dundonians) experience it. On our map, you can discover locally owned businesses, community spaces and initiatives, informal events only locals know about, open mic nights and local gigs, hidden gems, street art, pop-ups, significant sites of Dundonian history and culture, and spots around the city favoured by locals. Why only discover Dundee, when you could discover Dundonian?
